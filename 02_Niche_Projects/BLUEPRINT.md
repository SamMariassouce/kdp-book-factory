# BLUEPRINT.md

This blueprint outlines a 10-book portfolio strategy for a high-end wine-tasting journal brand. The strategy leverages the "Gold Mine" keywords identified and addresses specific gaps found in the competition data (e.g., the need for professional-grade interiors, hardcover durability, and educational "guide" components).

---

## **Book 1: The Luxury Flagship**
**Full SEO Title:** *Detailed Wine Tasting Journal Hardcover: The Professional Sommelier’s Archive & Wine Cellar Logbook*
*   **MARKET GAP:** Competition data shows the top-selling "Doug Paulding" journal is a cheap paperback at $8.36. This book wins by targeting the "Hardcover" ($17+ avg) and "Detailed" keywords with a premium, cloth-bound feel. It addresses the "Cellars" category where competition is low (15% large publisher presence).
*   **SPECS:** 
    *   **Page Count:** 200 pages (Higher than the standard 100-120).
    *   **Trim Size:** 6 x 9 inches.
    *   **Price:** $24.99.

## **Book 2: The Visual Masterclass**
**Full SEO Title:** *Wine Tasting Journal Guide: An Infographic Visual Companion for Sensory Discovery and Aroma Profiling*
*   **MARKET GAP:** Based on the "Wine Folly" keyword demand ($9,057/mo for the leader), this book will feature infographic interiors (aroma wheels, color scales). It bridges the gap between a blank logbook and a full textbook, satisfying the "educational value" sought in the "Guide" keyword.
*   **SPECS:** 
    *   **Page Count:** 160 pages.
    *   **Trim Size:** 8 x 10 inches (Larger for visual data).
    *   **Price:** $19.99.

## **Book 3: The Sommelier’s Standard**
**Full SEO Title:** *Wine Tasting Journal Sommelier Edition: Professional Technical Evaluation Log for Viticulture & Oenology*
*   **MARKET GAP:** Competition titles like "Sommelier in Training" exist but lack technical depth. This book will use specific prompts for acidity, tannins, ABV, and fermentation styles, targeting the high-authority niche with low competition results.
*   **SPECS:** 
    *   **Page Count:** 150 pages.
    *   **Trim Size:** 5.5 x 8.5 inches (Portable for floor work).
    *   **Price:** $16.95.

## **Book 4: The Collector’s Hardcover**
**Full SEO Title:** *Detailed Wine Tasting Journal: Premium Hardcover Reviewer for Wine Label Collecting & Tasting Adventures*
*   **MARKET GAP:** Data shows "Vino Memoirs" sells for label collecting. This book will combine "Hardcover" durability with dedicated spaces for label removal/pasting—a feature lacking in the high-volume/low-cost "Papeterie Bleu" journals.
*   **SPECS:** 
    *   **Page Count:** 120 pages (Thicker paper stock for labels).
    *   **Trim Size:** 8.25 x 11 inches.
    *   **Price:** $27.95.

## **Book 5: The Academic Workbook**
**Full SEO Title:** *The Wine Tasting Journal Guide & Workbook: A Guided Practice for Mock Exams and Sensory Mastery*
*   **MARKET GAP:** A competitor ("The Grape Edit") is successfully selling a "Mock Exam Workbook" for $19.99. We will improve on this by targeting "Study Guides" and "Education" categories identified in the analyzer, focusing on WSET/CMS exam preparation.
*   **SPECS:** 
    *   **Page Count:** 220 pages.
    *   **Trim Size:** 8.5 x 11 inches.
    *   **Price:** $22.50.

## **Book 6: The "Wine Folly" Alternative (Infographic Style)**
**Full SEO Title:** *Wine Tasting Journal: Infographic Tasting Notes & Aroma Chart Guide for Modern Wine Lovers*
*   **MARKET GAP:** Directly targets the "Wine Folly" keyword ratio. While "Wine Folly" is a massive 320-page guide, there is a gap for a *pure journal* that uses their same approachable, infographic aesthetic for recording personal notes.
*   **SPECS:** 
    *   **Page Count:** 130 pages.
    *   **Trim Size:** 7.5 x 9.25 inches.
    *   **Price:** $14.99.

*   **MARKET GAP:** Competition data shows "Napa Valley" and "Temecula" specific journals. We win by positioning this as a "Detailed" journal for "Specialty Travel" (Category 14), which has an 89% large publisher presence, signaling a highly profitable professional niche.

    *   **Page Count:** 100 pages.
    *   **Trim Size:** 5 x 8 inches (Travel-ready pocket size).
